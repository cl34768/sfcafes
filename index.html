SF Cafes

  • Cavalli Cafe: Open to 9pm; great, lively environment; americano tastes burnt af; desserts are amazing (canoli is citrusy and triamisue is fresh)
  • Blue Bottle (2 S Park St): Open to 5pm; cozy with a very relaxed environment; quality coffees; food options are bare bones
  • Compton Coffee House: Open to 5pm; small, quant shop; iced americano was very good; limited food menu
  • Peet's (Fillmore in Pac Heights): Open to 7pm; lots of old people but really spacious; only got the chamomille tea (not great); food options are your chain coffee shop options
  • Cafe Reveille: Open to 7pm; currently outdoor seating only; really good americano; burger is okay (veggies in burger are MASSIVE but not really flavorful)
